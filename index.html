How to install?

  1. Download all packs from this site.
  2. Unzip all packs.
  3. Turn off Call of Juarez if it is still running.
  4. Paste all unziped folders to Call of Juarez\Data\mapsnet directory:
    CD version path should be like: %ProgramFiles%\Techland\Call of Juarez\Data\mapsnet
    And Steam version: %ProgramFiles%\Steam\steamapps\common\Call of Juarez\Data\mapsnet
  5. Turn on your favourite game and the maps will be available :).
  6. Congratulations, now you can join.

More information about installation you can find in the Steam community.
